Jichao Zhang

Jichao Zhang is an Assistant Professor (Lecturer) with School of Computer Science, Ocean University of China. He received the Ph.D. degree from Multimedia and Human Understanding Group at the University of Trento advised by Prof.Nicu Sebe . His research interests include deep generative model, 2D image and 3D Avatar generation and editing.
